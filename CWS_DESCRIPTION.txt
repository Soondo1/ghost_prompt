Prompt Ghost - Real-Time AI Prompt Optimizer

Supercharge your LLM prompts in real-time. Prompt Ghost runs quietly in the background on your favorite chat interfaces, providing instant, AI-optimized suggestions to make your prompts more concise, detailed, and effective.

FEATURES:
- Real-Time Suggestions: As you type in any LLM textarea, Prompt Ghost analyzes your input and suggests an optimized version in-place.
- In-Place Autocomplete: Simply press Tab to accept the optimized suggestion and replace your text.
- Dual Transformation: Toggle between concise and detailed variations of your prompts in the popup interface.
- Prompt History: Keep track of your past queries, optimized variations, and acceptance rates with a built-in History feed.
- Usage Tracking: View your daily API limits, tokens consumed, and quota metrics with visual progress bars.

SUPPORTED PLATFORMS:
- ChatGPT (chatgpt.com)
- Claude AI (claude.ai)
- Gemini (gemini.google.com)
- Perplexity (perplexity.ai)
- DeepSeek (deepseek.com)
- Poe (poe.com)
- Microsoft Copilot
- Hugging Face Chat
- Meta AI

HOW TO USE:
1. Load the extension and sign up / log in via the popup window.
2. Navigate to any supported LLM platform.
3. Start typing your prompt. A "ghost" suggestion will appear showing the optimized prompt.
4. Press `Tab` to accept the suggestion instantly, or keep typing to ignore it.
5. Open the popup to browse your prompt history, configure optimization levels, or monitor your remaining daily quota.

Privacy first: Your prompt text is transmitted securely over HTTPS and is never shared or sold to third parties.
